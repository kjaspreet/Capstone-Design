All You Need in Just One App

Sometimes it's hard to find a single platform where you can chat, make audio/video calls, share files and screen with your classmates. ConnectUs provides you all these features. You can send one to one text messages and can make audio/video calls. If you missed any class then you can receive/send class notes from/to your classmates. Also, if you are facing any issue/difficulty then you can clarify your doubts by sharing your screen with your classmates.
